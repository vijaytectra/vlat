VLAT Exam Structure

for academic year 2026 - 27

Understanding the structure of the VMRF Law Admission Test (VLAT) is the first step towards preparing confidently. The exam has been designed to be straightforward, student-friendly, and aligned with the core skills required in law school.

VLAT focuses only on what truly matters for a law aspirant — your ability to comprehend, analyse, and reason logically. There is no rote learning, no memorisation of legal knowledge, and no trick questions.
