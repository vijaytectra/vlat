VLAT Exam Structure

for academic year 2026 - 27

Registering for the VMRF Law Admission Test (VLAT) is simple, quick, and completely online. Whether you are applying for a five-year integrated law programme, the three-year LL.B., or the LL.M., the entire registration process can be completed from your home within minutes.
