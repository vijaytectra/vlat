About Vinayaka Mission's Law School

The Law School of the Future

Vinayaka Mission's Law School was founded with a clear purpose: to build a world-class, accessible, and future-oriented law school that brings global excellence to every learner. Since 2021, VMLS has grown under the academic mentorship of Jindal Global Law School and the leadership of faculty trained at internationally renowned universities.

Our bilingual English–Tamil learning model ensures that legal education is not only rigorous and contemporary, but also inclusive—strengthening comprehension, breaking access barriers, and empowering students from diverse backgrounds. With a curriculum anchored in innovation, ethics, and experiential learning, VMLS prepares graduates to navigate the evolving landscape of law with clarity, skill, and purpose.

About VMLS

Shaping transformative legal education through innovation, inclusivity, and global vision. Vinayaka Mission's Law School (VMLS), established by Vinayaka Mission's Research Foundation – Deemed to be University (VMRF-DU), is dedicated to delivering legal education that responds to the rapidly evolving needs of local, national, and global communities. Founded with the vision of creating the law school of the future, VMLS welcomed its first cohort in 2021. Since then, mentorship from Jindal Global Law School and a committed team of full-time faculty—many trained at globally renowned institutions—have shaped a distinctive academic environment grounded in innovation and forward-looking pedagogy.

With state-of-the-art smart classrooms, a digitally enabled library, and a regal moot court hall, the campus provides an immersive learning atmosphere that encourages intellectual growth and active engagement. As a bilingual law school in Chennai, VMLS offers legal education in both English and Tamil, equipping students for global legal careers while remaining rooted in regional and cultural contexts. VMLS proudly upholds inclusivity as a core value, ensuring that every learner is supported and empowered to thrive.
