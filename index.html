About Vinayaka Mission's Law School

Building India's premier law school for the AI age through innovation, excellence, and inclusivity.

With the founding vision of creating the law school of the future, VMLS admitted its first cohort of students in 2021. Mentored by India's finest law school, Jindal Global Law School, and supported by a committed team of full-time faculty members trained in globally renowned institutions, VMLS has pioneered pedagogic innovations that equip learners with the skills, knowledge, and specializations required for professional success in the modern legal landscape.

Registration Process

VLAT 2026

STEP (01)

Start Registration

Begin by creating your account using your personal email ID and basic contact information.

STEP (02)

Fill in Your Details

Complete the VLAT application form by entering your personal, academic, and required information.

STEP (03)

Upload Documents

Attach all mandatory documents such as ID proof, academic records, and photographs.

STEP (04)

Pay & Submit

Pay the application fee securely online.After successful payment, submit your application form and download the receipt for future reference.
