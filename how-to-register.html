How to Register for VLAT?

for academic year 2026 - 27

Registering for the Vinayaka Mission's Law Admission Test(VLAT) is simple, quick, and completely online.
Whether you are applying for a five-year integrated law programme, the three-year LL.B., or the LL.M., the entire
registration process can be completed from your home within minutes.

Steps to Register for VLAT 2026

STEP 1

1. Visit the Official VMLS Website

Go to the official Vinayaka Mission's Law School website and navigate to the VLAT Admissions / Apply Now section.

This is where all updates, deadlines, and the registration link are available.

STEP 2

2. Create Your VLAT Account

Click on Register and create your account using:
• A valid email ID
• An active mobile number

You will receive important exam notifications, login information, and result updates through these details, so ensure they are correct.

STEP 3

3. Fill Out the Online Application Form

Once your account is created, log in and complete the VLAT application form.

You will be asked to provide:
• Personal details
• Academic qualifications
• Address and contact information
• Programme you wish to apply for (BA LL.B., B.Com LL.B., BBA LL.B., LL.B., or LL.M.)

Take your time and review each field to avoid mistakes.

STEP 4

4. Upload Required Documents

You will need to upload scanned copies of:
• A recent passport-size photograph
• Signature
• Academic certificates (Class 12 / Graduation / LL.B. depending on programme)
• Any ID proof if required

Make sure all files are clear and meet the size/format guidelines mentioned on the portal.

STEP 5

5. Pay the Application Fee

The payment is made online through secure digital gateways.

• ₹1000 for UG Programmes (BA LL.B., B.Com LL.B., BBA LL.B., LL.B.)
• ₹1500 for PG (LL.M.) Programme

Once the payment is successful, you will receive a receipt and confirmation email.

STEP 6

6. Submit Your Application

Before submitting the final form:
• Double-check your name, email, and academic details
• Ensure all documents are uploaded correctly
• Review the programme selection

Once submitted, you will receive a confirmation email with your application number. Keep this safe for future reference.

Tips for Smooth Registration

• Use a laptop or desktop for filling the form

• Keep all documents ready before starting

• Use a stable internet connection

• Check the eligibility criteria before applying

• Save screenshots or PDFs of the payment confirmation

• Save your application number for future reference
